Cookies

At La Vie en Provence, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owners.

Cookies can be classified as:

  • Session Cookies: These are temporary cookies that expire once you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: These remain on your device until they expire or are deleted.
  • First-party Cookies: These are set by the website you’re visiting.
  • Third-party Cookies: These are set by a third party, such as an analytics or advertising service.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for a variety of reasons, including:

  • To ensure the proper functioning of our website
  • To remember your preferences and personalize your experience
  • To analyze website traffic and improve the site’s performance
  • To offer relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns

The cookies we use fall into the following categories:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. Without them, some parts of the site may not be usable.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our site, such as which pages are visited most often, to help us improve its performance.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow the site to remember choices you make, such as language settings, to enhance your experience.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver ads that are more relevant to you and your interests. They may also limit the number of times you see an ad.

3. Third-Party Cookies

In some cases, we use third-party cookies provided by trusted services. For example, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. These third-party cookies are subject to the privacy policies of the third-party providers.

4. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies have been set
  • Delete cookies
  • Block cookies from being set

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and some features may not work properly.

We reserve the right to update or modify this Cookie Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed about our use of cookies.
