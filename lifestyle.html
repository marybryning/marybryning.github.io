Lifestyle

Lifestyle

Alongside its majestic lavender fields, Provence is a sun-soaked region famous for its easygoing lifestyle. Life here moves at a slower pace, inviting visitors to savor each moment. Whether you’re sitting down for an apéritif, wandering through a market, or enjoying a sunset stroll, the Provençal way of life reminds us all to slow down and embrace the beauty of the present.

Apéritif

One of the most cherished rituals in Provence is l’apéritif, a pre-dinner social gathering where friends and family come together to unwind. Typically held in the early evening, the "apéritif" is much more than a quick drink—it's a time to bond, chat, and relax. Locals gather at café terraces or in their homes, sipping on local favorites like pastis, an anise-flavored spirit, or a crisp rosé wine, which the region is famous for.

Paired with simple yet delicious nibbles like olives, tapenade (an olive spread), or slices of saucisson (cured sausage), the apéritif captures the essence of Provençal life: good company, local flavors, and the enjoyment of the present moment.

Provençal Markets

Another centerpiece of Provencal life are the many vibrant markets hosted across the region which offer stalls brimming with fresh produce, handmade cheeses, fragrant herbs, and local wines and olive oils. These markets are more than just a place to shop, they are a social hub which unites communities.

To best experience the local lifestyle, spend the morning perusing the markets and stop for a coffee in a café. One of the best markets the region has to offer are those of Aix-en-Provence which take place every Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday, transforming the town into a vibrant and bustling social space.

Flâner

Another provençal tradition is flâner, or leisurely strolling through their picturesque villages. It’s not uncommon to see families wandering along cobblestone streets, chatting with neighbors, or stopping for a final drink at the local café. The slow pace and communal feel make every evening in Provence a celebration of life’s simple pleasures.
